The Problem

Youth Soccer Is Broken

Clubs sell promises. Families pay thousands. And when it doesn't work out, players are left scrambling with no system to find what's next. There's no centralized platform connecting players to opportunities — just Google searches and word of mouth.

False Promises

Clubs sell a bag of goods — "play for us, we'll get you to the next level." But how many kids actually reap the benefits? The system favors the clubs, not the players.

No Visibility

Talented players have no centralized place to showcase their highlights, stats, and potential. Scouts and clubs can't find what they can't see.

Relocation Nightmare

Families move and have zero resources to find clubs in their new area. They rely on Google. No platform exists to search by location, level, and age group.

Year-Round Movement

Player movement isn't bounded by tryouts. Mid-season transfers, ID clinics, and new arrivals happen year-round — but there's no system for it.

"Each player has a ball at their feet and gets to decide their own future."

The Platform

LinkedIn for Youth Soccer

An all-in-one platform where players build profiles, clubs discover talent, and families find the right fit — powered by AI matching and built for every level from recreational to MLS.

⚽

Player Profiles

Highlights, stats, contact info, position, age, location — everything a club needs to evaluate a player, all in one place. Video hosting built in.

🔍

AI Club Discovery

"I'm in 8th grade, I live in Danbury, and I want to play ECNL within 20 miles." The AI matches players to clubs based on location, skill level, and preferences.

🔁

Transfer Market

Year-round, open platform. Clubs post needs ("We need a U14 forward"), players post availability. Mid-season transfers, ID clinics, and relocations — all covered.

📧

Direct Communication

Players reach out to clubs. Clubs reach out to players. No middlemen. Emails, profiles, and scouting event submissions — all from one dashboard.

🔒

Parent-First Privacy

Built on educational data privacy standards. Parent permission systems for all minors. Families control what's shared and with whom.

🏆

All Levels Welcome

MLS academies, ECNL, travel, and recreational. From the kid who just moved to town to the elite prospect going pro — one platform for everyone.

Revenue Model

Three Revenue Streams

Free to join. Premium features to stand out. A business model that grows with the player base.

Stream 01

Premium Profiles

Players and families pay to boost visibility — like Instagram promotion. Tiered pricing: basic profile (free) vs. enhanced profile (featured placement, priority in search, video highlights pinned).

Stream 02

Sponsor Advertising

Thousands of players on one platform = valuable real estate for soccer brands, equipment companies, camps, and academies. Get the players first, sponsors follow.

Stream 03

Club Data Access

Clubs pay for premium search and filtering — find specific player types by position, age, location, and skill level. The data becomes the product.
