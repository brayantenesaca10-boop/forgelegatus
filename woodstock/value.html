Layer 2: Capabilities You Don't Have Today

What Becomes Possible

These aren't time savings. These are things that are currently impossible — no matter how many hours you work.

Institutional Memory

Every meeting, every decision, every conversation — captured, searchable, connected. When a board member asks "didn't we discuss this last October?" you have the answer in seconds. Not in your head. In the system.

Personalized Communication at Scale

Draft 30 unique parent emails about 30 different students, each with specific context. Send thoughtful follow-ups to every teacher after an observation. Impossible by hand. Trivial with Jarvis.

Pattern Recognition Across Months

"What topics keep surfacing in meetings that we haven't resolved?" "Which action items keep getting pushed?" AI surfaces patterns your memory can't hold. Better information means better decisions.

Decision Support with Full Context

"Before I respond to this parent, what do I know about this family? What have we discussed before?" Jarvis holds the context so you can make the right call, not just a fast one.
